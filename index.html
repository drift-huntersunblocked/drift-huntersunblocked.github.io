Drift Hunters Tips and Strategy

The fastest improvement usually comes from learning one map and one car deeply instead of switching constantly. Consistent drift angles and cleaner exits will beat messy aggression.

  • Pick one car and learn how it behaves before spending time tuning everything.
  • Enter corners with intention; random throw-ins usually kill speed and angle.
  • Focus on smooth transitions rather than maximum steering input.
